Skills and Jobs
When our project has the opportunity to be interested in venture capitalists, we will develop the project's operation team as well as optimize the effectiveness of this project. Then we will need five people with different positions to also come together to take care of all the requirements and problems of the project.
  1. Project manager
    2. This is an important and indispensable position in a project implementation team. For this job, the person in charge must have a good leadership attitude, stay calm, and see everything thoroughly and transparently. In addition to the must-have elements of a leader, in terms of expertise, the manager must be able to manage all the technical stages in this project from the point of view and always follow up with other positions. in the team to synchronize the content together to help the project have consistency and high efficiency. This position prioritizes leaders with experience in IT that easily support the interaction between members.
  2. UI/UX - Front-end Developer
    3. This position represents the 'face' of the project. So it requires a person who is creative in terms of art through which they can convey the ideas of the whole group to the user. In addition to being creative, this position also encourages the holder to know about front-end programming. It is possible to convert innovative ideas to programming software to complete the website. And this position requires the holder to know HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and some related programming languages. The person in charge of this position also has to understand the entire project well and figure out how to communicate it in a way that is understandable and attractive to users.
  3. Back - end Developer
    4. This position requires knowledge of databases such as MySQL. These are the most essential tools to help a website function properly and can store data. Besides, Back-end Developer needs to communicate well with Front-end Developer to unite the project tasks and develop new ideas. In addition, this is an important position for the security of the project, so it is necessary to have careful skills at work and always ensure all personal information of users and data of the project. The core of the project is absolute security
  4. Tester
    5. The Tester will comprehensively evaluate the project after the programmers complete it. This position requires the person in charge to use and research all the existing features of the project and try to uncover bugs and circumstances that cause problems affecting the website, such as delays, data congestion, display errors, and other related errors. In addition, the Tester has to accompany the Back-end and Front-end Developers to find solutions to the arising problems. In addition, for our project, the Tester will be the person who will always support the users when they have issues because the Tester is the one who best understands the errors in the program and will have appropriate plans to help users quickly and accurately.
  5. Marketing and Finance
    6. This job requires a person with knowledge of economics and markets. This location is vital in spreading our project to all students on campus and creating a clean, transparent, and friendly ecosystem. In addition, this job also requires the person in charge to balance the cash flow to maintain paid services such as renting a web domain, renting a server, paying for co-developers as well, and offering an appropriate valuation. to reach agreements with venture capitalists to develop more complete and widely available products jointly.

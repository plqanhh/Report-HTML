Team Profile
  1. Team name
    2. After many discussions, we came up with the final team name: TinMate due to many reasons. Firstly, since our project is inspired by Tinder, we want to give our project a name that has similarities with that dating app. Secondly, the name has a specific meaning, representing our team essence. Before working as a team, we did not know each other, and each member has different schools, majors, years of study and ages. We understand that everyone has their own strengths and weaknesses, and the weakness of an individual can be the strength of someone else in the group. Hence, by communicating effectively about our abilities, we are able to allocate tasks to suit everyone, allowing each person to contribute their best to the projects. By that, we build trust in each other, which helps us to deliver the best result on this project.

  3. Group Process
      4. Our group has worked quite well together due to effective project management. Firstly, task allocation is decided based on individual strengths. Tony is in charge of managing and keeping track of product backlog as well as sprint backlog. Quynh Anh and Phuoc design the website interface by Figma, while Tuan Anh is mainly responsible for building a user interface in Python with support from Quynh Anh and Tony. Finally, Phuoc will double-check if the website works as expected.To keep track of the project’s flow, our team has a weekly meeting on Friday. In the meeting, each member will update about the progress and difficulties if having. By doing that, the team can support and find the solution for problems as soon as possible. Meeting agenda and meeting minutes will be recorded by Quynh Anh, and then uploaded on Trello and Google Drive for other team members to view later.
